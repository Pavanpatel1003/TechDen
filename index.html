Flexible Membership Options

Choose a plan that fits your growth stage — from single founders to full
startup teams.

Starter

$0 per user/month, billed annually

For Solo founders & early-stage innovators

Shared workspace access
(midday to evening hours)
Community event invites and online workshops
Monthly group mentorship sessions
Wi-Fi, printing, and coffee bar

Explorer

$100 per user/month, billed annually

For founders building prototypes and growing small teams.

Flexible desk in co-working area, 24/7 access
Access to prototyping labs and maker equipment (limited hours)
Monthly 1:1 mentorship and office hours
Team collaboration rooms (4 hours / month)

Growth

$200 per user/month, billed annually

Designed for teams needing dedicated space and enhanced support.

Private office space (2-5 members)
Full access to labs and fabrication resources
Unlimited meeting room bookings
Accelerated mentorship, investor pitch prep
Dedicated account manager and support

Enterprise

Custom pricing per user/month, billed annually

For growing startups with complex needs and enterprise partnerships.

Scalable private office suites and reserved desks
Customized program integration & corporate innovation services
Reserved lab and equipment priority booking
Onsite technical and business support teams
Partner networking and co-marketing opportunities
