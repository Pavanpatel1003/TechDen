What is included?

Our programs include access to state-of-the-art facilities, expert mentorship, funding opportunities, networking events, and comprehensive business development support.

How does selection work?

Our selection process involves application review, initial screening, pitch presentation, and final interviews with our expert panel. We evaluate based on innovation potential, team strength, and market opportunity.

What support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive support including mentorship, workspace access, prototyping facilities, funding opportunities, networking events, and business development resources.

How long are the programs?

Program duration varies by type: HardTech Incubator is 6 months, IoT Accelerator is 4 months, while Mentorship Network and Community Programs are ongoing with flexible participation.

Do you take equity?

Equity requirements vary by program. Some programs are equity-free, while others may take a small equity stake in exchange for funding and intensive support. Details are provided during the application process.
